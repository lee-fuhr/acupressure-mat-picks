Acupressure mats use hundreds of small spikes to apply pressure to trigger points along your back, neck, and feet. The idea is simple: lie down on the mat for 15–30 minutes to release tension, improve circulation, and ease muscle soreness. Whether you’re dealing with chronic back pain, stress, or just want a drug-free way to unwind, a good mat can become a daily ritual. When choosing a mat, pay attention to spike density, material quality, and included accessories like a pillow. Some mats are firmer, some more forgiving. A thicker foam base tends to be more comfortable for beginners, while a tightly packed spike layout offers deeper acupressure.
